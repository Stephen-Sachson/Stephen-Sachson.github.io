Privacy Policy — Abjad日本語

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Abjad日本語 ("the App") is a Japanese input method designed to provide a minimal and efficient transliteration experience. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App is designed with privacy first. It does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • No personal data
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No keystrokes or entered text
  • No usage analytics or tracking data
  • No location information

All typing and transliteration processing occurs locally on your device. Nothing you type ever leaves your device.

3. Data the App May Access

The App may access certain system resources solely to function properly:

  • Keyboard permissions — Required to allow the input method to operate.

No data accessed through these permissions is stored or transmitted.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external servers.

No data is shared with any third party.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App collects no personal data and is safe for users of all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Any changes will be available on this page with an updated date.
